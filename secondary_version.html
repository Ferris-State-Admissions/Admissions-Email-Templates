Ferris State University offers you the chance to pay in-state tuition at an out-of-state university. This is possible with our Great Lakes Scholarship.

This scholarship is available to first-time students and transfer students who live, or attend a university, in one of the eligible states.

The Great lakes Scholarship can be renewed up to three times and combined with other scholarships.
